MMMMM... Brownies

Ingredients needed:

  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 ½ cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ⅔ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon baking soda

Directions for baking:

  1. Before all, preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) and grease an 8-inch square pan
  2. After preheating the oven to 350 degrees F (equivalent to 175 degrees C) combine sugar, butter, and water in a medium saucepan; cook and stir over medium heat until boiling
  3. Remove from heat and stir in chocolate chips until melted and smooth; mix in eggs and vanilla.
  4. Combine flour, salt, and baking soda; stir into the chocolate mixture.
  5. Spread brownie batter evenly into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away from the sides of the pan, about 20 to 30 minutes.
  7. Let cool completely before cutting into squares.
  8. Enjoy!
